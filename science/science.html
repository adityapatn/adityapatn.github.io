2023 National Science Bowl

In 7th grade, I took the qualifying test for the Evergreen Middle School National Science Bowl teams and placed on Team 1 by scoring top four in the entire school. Our team of four studied weekly for several hours to prepare for the competition, where we placed 2nd during qualification matches but were knocked out in our fourth round of elimination matches.

2024 National Science Bowl

In 8th grade, I took the qualifying test for the Evergreen Middle School National Science Bowl teams and placed on Team 1 for the second year in a row by scoring top four in the entire school. Our team of four studied weekly for several hours to prepare for the competition, where we placed 1st during qualification matches but were knocked out in the semifinals of elimination matches.
